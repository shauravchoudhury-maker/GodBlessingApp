    Verify & export

    Review the verses & captions above. For translated posts, use the Verify meaning button in the Daily Studio first. When you're happy, export the bundle and import it into your scheduler.

    The bundle contains schedule.csv (one row per post: date, time, platform, caption, hashtags, media file), one image per post, and a README. Import schedule.csv into a bulk scheduler — Buffer, Publer, Metricool, or Hootsuite — connect your Instagram/TikTok/Facebook/YouTube/X accounts there once, and it auto-publishes at the times you set. Your platform logins stay inside your scheduler; this app never sees or stores them. So your only steps are: verify → import → it posts automatically.

## Why this line-up works
- **Theme mix hits the biggest save/share niches:** self-worth, anxiety/rest, stoicism, burnout, self-care, kindness, comfort.
- **Faith rotation** (2 affirmations · 2 Bible · 1 Gita · 1 Stoic · 1 Buddhist) shows your unique multi-faith range — no competitor covers this spread.
- **Format variety** (4 carousels · 1 single · 1 Reel · 1 carousel) keeps the feed dynamic and tells the algorithm you use every surface.
- **Every caption ends with a save + share + follow prompt** — the three actions that drive reach and growth.

1. Generate in the studio (keep the **signature editorial + grain** look on).
2. Post **same time daily** (consistency > perfection).
3. First 30 min: reply to every comment (boosts ranking).
4. Add the post to your **Story** with a "new post 👇" sticker.
5. Cross-post the same file to **Facebook** + pin slide 1 to **Pinterest**.
